What is 0x80?

0x80 is a simple to use free file hosting service. It lets you share your photos, documents, music, videos and more with others online for 24 hours.

Is 0x80 Pomf?

Yes/No. The site is based around pomf's "simple sharing" aspect, but no code comes from pomf.

Banned Content

But what if I really wanna upload [insert banned content]?

What does mean file stored Permanently?

We use IPFS to distribute files and make them permanent, and to keep them available even when this website is down.

Why I can't see IPFS link or CID after upload?

IPFS link or CID only get from API.

Can you remove illegal files?

Unfortunately not because all uploaded files will be distributed and permanent, we recommend not uploading illegal files.
